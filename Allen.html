About:

In 1988, Shri Rajesh Maheshwari started with just eight students and after a few months Dr. K.G.Vaishnava, the eminent professor of Biology also joined him. It was the only institute of its time which provided coaching for all Science subjects i.e. Physics,Chemistry, Biology and Mathematics under one roof. This strong association became the foundation stone of the revolutionary Pre-Medical coaching institute of that time.Meanwhile, Er. Brajesh Maheshwari, a dynamic person with excellent engineering background joined the team.The past achievements were complemented with remarkable progress when our Classroom Coaching Students bagged the All India First Ranks in both the prestigious examinations of India, JEE Advanced 2014 and AIPMT 2014 respectively. In 2016, ALLEN broke it's own stupendous records of previous year results by becoming the first ever Institute of the country to produce All India Ranks 1,2,3 in both JEE Advanced and NEET- UG from classroom.
